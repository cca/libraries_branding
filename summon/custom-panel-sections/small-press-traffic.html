A collection of small press and handmade books of literature and poetry, donated by Small Press Traffic Literary Arts Center (SPT), an organization at the heart of San Francisco's innovative writing scene, bringing together readers, writers, and independent presses to engage, create, present, critique and archive their work. SPT's mission is to provide a local and national platform for experimental writing, paying particular attention to underserved writers and those that identify as women, LBGTQ and/or people of color.
